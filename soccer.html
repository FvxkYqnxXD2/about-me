Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is the most popular sport globally, with millions of fans and players.

History

The origins of soccer can be traced back to ancient civilizations, but the modern version of the game was codified in England in the 19th century. The first official rules were established by the Football Association in 1863.

Rules

The objective of soccer is to score more goals than the opposing team within a 90-minute match. The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. Players use their feet to kick the ball, and only the goalkeeper is allowed to use their hands within the penalty area.

Famous Tournaments

  • FIFA World Cup
  • UEFA Champions League
  • Copa America
  • Premier League
